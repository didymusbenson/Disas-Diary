# MTG Mana Colors Reference

Background colors for mana symbol circles, extracted from the standard MTG mana icon palette.

| Color     | Symbol | Hex       | Notes                          |
|-----------|--------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| MTG_WHITE     | W      | `#F9FAF4` | Pale cream                     |
| MTG_BLUE      | U      | `#AAE0FA` | Light sky blue                 |
| MTG_BLACK     | B      | `#A8A29E` | Warm medium grey               |
| MTG_RED       | R      | `#F9AA8F` | Salmon / peach                 |
| MTG_GREEN     | G      | `#9BD3AE` | Sage green                     |
| MTG_COLORLESS | C      | `#CBC2BF` | Cool light grey (distinct from black) |

Note: Black and Colorless are both grey but different shades -- Black is warmer and darker, Colorless is cooler and lighter.
