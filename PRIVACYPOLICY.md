# Privacy Policy for Disas Diary

**Last Updated: November 28, 2025**

## Overview

Disas Diary is a Magic: The Gathering companion app designed to help players track Tarmogoyf's power/toughness and graveyard card types during gameplay.

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or use any personal data whatsoever.**

- **No data collection**: The app does not collect any information about you or your device.
- **No analytics**: We do not use any analytics services to track app usage.
- **No third-party services**: The app does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect data.
- **Local storage only**: All game state (token counts, card type toggles, counter values) is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device.
- **No internet connection required**: The app functions entirely offline and does not communicate with any servers.
- **No advertising**: The app contains no advertisements or advertising frameworks.

## Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. By design, Disas Diary operates entirely on your device with no data leaving your device at any time.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices in the future, we will update this privacy policy and the "Last Updated" date above.
